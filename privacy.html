Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how applications published by KingNerd collect, use and protect your information. Our philosophy is simple: Your data belongs to you.

Information We Collect

Most KingNerd applications are designed to work completely offline. Unless explicitly stated inside an application, we do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Financial account credentials
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers

Information Stored on Your Device

Applications may store information locally on your own device, including:

  • Application settings
  • Portfolio data
  • Trading history
  • Notes
  • Property information
  • User preferences

This information remains on your device unless you explicitly export or back it up.

Internet Access

Some applications may access the internet only to provide requested functionality such as:

  • Retrieving financial market prices
  • Checking application updates
  • Validating software licenses
  • Restoring purchases

No unnecessary information is transmitted.

Google Play Billing

Paid features are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing. KingNerd never receives or stores your payment card information. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Depending on the application, the following third-party services may be used:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Yahoo Finance (market data)
  • GitHub Pages (legal documentation)

Analytics and Advertising

Unless specifically stated within an application:

  • No advertising
  • No behavioral tracking
  • No sale of personal information
  • No third-party analytics

Data Security

We strive to minimize data collection and follow security best practices. Whenever possible, your information remains stored only on your own device.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, including the European Union (GDPR), you may have rights regarding your personal information. Since most KingNerd applications do not collect personal information, these rights generally relate only to information you voluntarily provide (for example, when contacting support).

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this website.
