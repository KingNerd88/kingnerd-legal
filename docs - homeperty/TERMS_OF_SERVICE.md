## 1. The service

HomePerty is a property-management app for landlords and short-term-rental
hosts. It works in two modes:

- **Local mode** (default, available to everyone, including free-tier
  users): all data you enter is stored on your device only. No account is
  required.
- **Cloud Backup** (included with Pro): once you connect a Google
  account, the App **automatically** keeps backups of your data on
  Google Firebase — up to the last 10 versions — so you can restore on
  any device. Backups are encrypted in transit and at rest and are
  accessible only to your account. See §5 and the Privacy Policy for
  details.
