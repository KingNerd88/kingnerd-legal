Before Contacting Support

To help us solve your problem quickly, please include the following information:

  • Application name
  • Application version
  • Android version
  • Phone or tablet model
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots (if applicable)

Feature Requests

We love hearing new ideas. If you have suggestions for improving one of our applications, feel free to email us. Many new features are added based on user feedback.

Bug Reports

If you discover a bug, please provide as much detail as possible. Useful information includes:

  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Whether the problem can be reproduced
  • Any screenshots or error messages

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the application and use the Restore Purchases option in the Settings screen.

Do your applications collect personal information?

Most KingNerd applications are designed with privacy in mind and store your data locally whenever possible. Please see our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Do you provide financial advice?

No. Applications such as Theta Pilot are educational and portfolio management tools only. They do not provide investment advice.
